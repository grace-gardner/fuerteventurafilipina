Hello! I'm Grace, author of this blog and I am from the Philippines! We first visited Fuerteventura in 2018 and instantly fell in love with the island. The all year round sunshine and warm weather, the quality of life and the beautiful beaches are amongst the many reasons that really got us hooked. Since buying a property in 2021, we have been visiting every 6 to 8 weeks, mainly to explore more of the island but most importantly, to prepare for our planned relocation. With my husband being an Irish Citizen, our plan is to apply for his residency first - Tarjeta de Identification del Extranjero - TIE. Following that, our next plan of action is to apply for my EU family member residence card, which, at the time of writing this post, the EU family member residence card is valid for 5 years . The good news for me is that I only require 2 years of residency to be eligible to apply for Spanish Citizenship, all thanks to the Philippines being a Spanish colony many moons ago But that's neither here or now, so in the meantime, come and join us on our journey!
